Privacy.

Mixed-control variant of the A14 archetype — the screen that exercises the full control vocabulary. Radios for profile visibility and address granularity, a five-stop slider with live map preview for location fuzz, toggles for activity visibility, leading-icon chevron rows for data export. Two frames contrast everyday defaults against a Stealth state where every control is set to its most private.

top bar52px, chevron-left + centered title "Privacy". No trailing affordance.
radio cardsProfile visibility (4 options) & Address on profile (4 options) — Row + Radio. Sub on each option spells out the exact thing strangers will see.
step sliderFive-stop slider for "Map location fuzz": Exact · Block · Quarter mile · Half mile · Neighborhood. Filled track in primary-600, white thumb with 2px primary border.
fuzz mapInline 92px-tall map strip below the slider — faint street grid, central pin, blue fuzz circle that scales with the selected stop. Mono tag in the corner names the current stop.
toggle groupActivity card — 4 standard iOS toggles: online status, recent activity, nearby search, public ratings. Each sub tells you what the toggle exposes.
leading iconsData card rows carry primary-50 tinted 32px icon badges (download, file-text). Reserved for "actions" rather than "settings" — separates them from the radio/toggle vocabulary above.
stealth bannerDark slate card above the first overline — visible only on Frame 2. Eye-off icon, "Stealth mode is on" + sub explaining the floor it puts under everything.
helpersEach card's helper text rewrites between frames to reflect the chosen pattern's consequence — never just restating the toggle.
destructiveDetached "Delete account" card at the bottom with sub "Permanent. 30-day grace period." Present in both frames.
Two frames · populated · stealth (secondary)
FRAME 1 · POPULATED
Everyday defaults
Verified-only profile, street name on profile, "Block" location fuzz, balanced activity. The settings most people sit at after onboarding — open enough to be useful, private enough to feel safe.
FRAME 2 · STEALTH (SECONDARY)
Everything at the strictest
Stealth banner up top; profile hidden, address hidden, fuzz at Neighborhood (biggest blue circle on the map preview), every activity toggle off. Helpers shift to consequence language — "buyers see only Park Slope", "Existing chats still work".
