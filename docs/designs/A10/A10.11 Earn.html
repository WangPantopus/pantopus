Pantopus · Mobile · A10.11

Earn

The earn drawer of the digital mailbox — a dashboard for the side of Pantopus where neighbors make money. It shares the Wallet’s dark balance hero, but reframes it around momentum: a weekly-goal bar sits under the available amount, then a “ways to earn” card pushes the next paid task, an earnings list shows what’s landed, payout settings hold the bank + auto-cashout cadence, and a tax-doc link closes it out. Cash out is the sticky primary.

src/app/mailbox/earn.tsx

slot.header_pattern

balance_hero + goal — the same dark sky surface as Wallet, but Earn adds a weekly-goal progress bar (teal fill) beneath the available amount and a This week / Lifetime split. It answers “how am I doing this week,” not just “what can I move.”

slot.body_pattern

ways_to_earn · earnings · payout · taxes — a featured “Browse open tasks” row leads the ways-to-earn card; the earnings list is grouped-by-day with category-tinted icons and paid/pending status; payout settings pair the bank tile with an auto-cashout toggle; tax docs sit last.

slot.primary_cta

cash_out — sticky bar with the amount trailing the label so the number is confirmed before the tap. When there’s nothing to move, the control pivots to Browse open tasks with a one-line note that cash out unlocks after the first paid task.
