Pantopus · Mobile · A10.10

Wallet

Earnings wallet for neighbors getting paid through Pantopus gigs. The screen has one job above the fold: tell you, fast, how much you can move to your bank right now — and whether anything is in the way. Below the fold is the audit trail: recent activity, payout method, and a tax-docs entry that lights up around 1099 season. The financial-services adjacent vibe shows up in the dark-sky balance hero; everything else stays in the Pantopus calm-card vocabulary.

src/app/wallet.tsx

slot.header_pattern

balance_hero — the only dark surface in the app. Big USD amount, decorative concentric arcs (no marketing gradients elsewhere — this earns its weight), and a glass-strip splitting Pending from This month so the user always knows what’s actually withdrawable vs still in escrow.

slot.body_pattern

tx_list · payout_method · tax_docs — grouped-by-day transactions with category-tinted icons (cleaning green, child-care amber, handyman orange, pet-care red), each row showing the cleared / pending / fee status as a tiny chip on the right. Payout method is a debit-card-shaped tile; tax-docs row hides until there’s actual filing-worthy activity.

slot.primary_cta

withdraw — sticky bottom bar, full amount on the right of the button so the user confirms the number before tapping. In the hold state the same control swaps to a locked variant with a one-line policy footnote pointing at the re-verify card above; never an alert dialog.
