Invite to home

General invite — for residents, helpers, and guests. Differs from A13.5 Invite Owner: no ownership grant, no co-signer round-trip, role tiers are scoped instead of equity. Channel toggle picks email or SMS; the form validates the contact, surfaces a Pantopus match if found, and offers a free-text note before sending.
