Pantopus · A13.10 · Form (single screen)

Edit business page

Multi-section variant of the Form archetype, applied to the public-facing business profile. Owners scroll a long stack — banner & logo, name, description, hours, services, gallery, contact, location — each editable in-place. Save is sticky-bottom because edits are usually partial; the bar swaps between a "3 unsaved · Discard + Save" dirty state and a setup-mode "Save draft / Publish · 4 to go" pair.
