My Mail Day

Simple variant of the Form archetype, used as a daily review editor. Each "field" is a routing decision against a piece of physical mail the household scanned today. The screen accumulates decisions top-down: items needing a call sit at the top with a suggested recipient and one-tap Route; everything you've routed is collapsed into a compact reviewed list below, with an active 5-second undo on the latest. A scan card injects more mail mid-day; "Finish day" closes the ledger.
