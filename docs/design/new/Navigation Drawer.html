Pantopus · Mobile Archetype · Navigation Drawer

The context drawer — one menu, three identities.

A left panel sliding over a dimmed scrim, opened from the Hub menu. It is context-aware: its body sections rewrite themselves for the active context — Personal, a specific Home, or a specific Business — and its header doubles as the identity/context switcher. The contract is this single panel and its four states.

Header is the switcher. Collapsed, it shows a context pill tinted by the active identity pillar. Tap the chevron and it expands into a full switcher — Personal, Professional, your homes, your businesses, and the two "add" rows. The body below always reflects whichever context is active.

Anatomy

Panel is ~82% of the bezel width over a rgba(0,0,0,.45) scrim. Tap the scrim or swipe left to dismiss. Surface --app-surface, elevation --shadow-xl.

context_pillRound pillar icon + label + chevron, on the matching -bg tint. Chevron-down collapsed, chevron-up expanded.
switcher_dropdownPersonal · Professional, then YOUR HOMES and YOUR BUSINESSES groups, each with an "+ add" dashed row. Active context carries a check.
context_sections[]Overline-titled groups of full-width rows (leading Lucide icon + label, ≥44pt tap target). The set swaps per context.
selected_rowCurrent area marked with --color-primary-50 fill + primary text + a trailing dot — mirrors how the Hub marks the active surface.
back_to_hubHome/Business only. A quiet-but-clear card pinned at the bottom that returns to Personal/Hub.
no_tab_barThe drawer overlays the tabbed shell — bottom tabs are never shown inside it.
