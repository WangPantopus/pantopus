Your audience — the people behind your Beacon.

The creator-side members screen, sibling to A22.1 Audience (the broadcast hub). List-of-Rows archetype: join requests to approve or decline sit above your active members, grouped by tier. Member rows reuse the A10.8 membership styling. Pushed sub-route: back chevron, no tab bar.

Inherits List-of-Rows + the beacon-family shell. Same bezel, status bar, and avatar as Following / A03.2. Data from GET /me/audience; Approve/Decline hit PATCH /me/audience/:membershipId. Filter chips switch scope (All · Pending · per-tier); tier overlines + pills are tinted with the membership tier tokens (VIP gold, Insiders silver).
Four frames · populated · filtered to pending · empty · no pending
FRAME 1 · POPULATED
Requests + tiered members
Pending requests (2, one verified-Local, each with Approve / Decline) above two tier groups — VIP (incl. a muted member) and Insiders. All-scope filter selected.
FRAME 2 · FILTERED
Pending only
"Pending · 2" chip selected (primary fill); members hidden. Quiet helper line explains that declining is silent.
FRAME 3 · EMPTY
No audience yet
Full-empty: identity-tinted users glyph, "No audience yet", explainer, primary "Share your Beacon", and a growth hint. Count line reads 0 members.
FRAME 4 · NO PENDING
Inline empty request slot
Members present but the queue is clear: a dashed "No pending requests" card sits where the request rows would be. The second empty state.
