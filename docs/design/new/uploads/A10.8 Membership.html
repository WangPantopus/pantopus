Fan membership

The fan-side manage screen for a paid membership to a persona (creator / business). Per audience-profile §11.6, this screen is the policy commitment made visible: the tier, what it promises, the renewal mechanics, and — critically — a single-tap cancel with no retention dark patterns. When the persona breaks an SLA, the screen owns the refund offer up front rather than burying it.

src/app/audience/membership/[personaId]/index.tsx

slot.header_pattern

tier_card — silver-tone strip with tier name, "2 of 3" ladder pill, monthly price big-right. Renewal row + payment row underneath. Reads like a paper membership card; no gradient hero.

slot.body_pattern

persona · benefits · inbox — persona card up top so you remember who you're paying; verified benefit list with the SLA promise inline ("Reply within 48h · SLA"); inbox row with unread dot. Change tier modal opens as a bottom sheet — same row layout but each tier carries an upgrade/downgrade arrow and the current row is locked.

slot.primary_cta · cancel

change_tier is the sky CTA; cancel_membership is a quiet text button with the Pantopus policy footnote ("Single-tap cancel. No retention questions."). One tap → confirm sheet → done. No win-back screen.

FRAME 1 · POPULATED
Silver tier, renews in 22 days, 1 unread from Lara
Happy path. Persona → tier card → benefits (with the SLA promise shown as a benefit, not buried in TOS) → Change tier primary → inbox row → cancel link + policy footnote.
FRAME 2 · SECONDARY · SLA MISSED
Lara owes you a reply — refund offered
Amber banner pinned above the fold owns the situation: states the broken promise, names the days, presents the refund as the primary action. "Give it a week" is the gentler choice — never a guilt-trip "Are you sure".
