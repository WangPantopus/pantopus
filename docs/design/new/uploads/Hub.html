Pantopus · Mobile Archetype · A02 · Tab

Hub — home command center

The most visually prominent tab in the app. Composes 9 sections as a single scrollable canvas — greeting, quick actions, setup banner, Today, four pillar cards, Discover, Jump-back-in, Activity. One shell covering (tabs)/index.tsx and control-center.tsx.

Section sequence

Each section occupies a fixed vertical slot. Empty sections collapse entirely (no "nothing here yet" placeholders) — the next section slides up to take its place.
