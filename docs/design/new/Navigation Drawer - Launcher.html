Pantopus · Mobile Archetype · Navigation Drawer · Variant B

Thin launcher — switching lives in the Identity Center.

Same context-aware menu, different switching path. Here the drawer is a launcher: the context pill doesn’t expand inline — tapping it hands off to the existing Identity Center switch surface. The drawer stays focused on navigation; one canonical screen owns identity and context.

One switcher, one place. The pill carries a “Switch” affordance instead of a chevron. Tapping it opens the Identity Center sheet — radio cards for Personal, Professional, your homes and businesses, plus a path to manage profiles & privacy. No switcher logic is duplicated inside the drawer.

Which fit is better?

This · Launcher

Drawer launches Identity Center

  • Single source of truth for switching — no drift between two switchers.
  • Room for richer switch UI: radio cards, privacy entry, “what people see”.
  • Drawer stays simple and fast to scan.
  • Cost: switching is a full screen change — one extra hop, loses drawer context.
Variant A · Inline

Switcher expands in the drawer

  • Switch without leaving the drawer — fewer taps, keeps context.
  • Feels native to a navigation drawer pattern.
  • Cost: a second switcher to keep in sync with the Identity Center.
  • See Variant A — inline switcher.
