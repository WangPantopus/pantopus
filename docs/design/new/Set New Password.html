Four frames · form → validation → success
FRAME 1 · DEFAULT
Awaiting input
Both fields empty, strength meter idle with its helper rule, “Update password” desaturated and locked until valid.
FRAME 2 · VALIDATION
Strong · confirm mismatch
New password revealed in mono, meter at “Strong” (3 green bars). Confirm doesn’t match — 1.5px red border, semantic fill, alert-circle message. Button stays locked.
FRAME 3 · VALID
Matched · ready to submit
Both fields green with trailing checks, “Passwords match”, and the primary CTA lit with its brand halo — the live target.
FRAME 4 · SUCCESS
“Password updated”
Green check halo, confirmation copy, an info chip noting other devices were signed out, and a primary “Continue to sign in” with trailing arrow.
Validation vocabulary — inherited from the Auth form
  • Default → focus: border lifts to primary-600 with a 3px halo; no fill change.
  • Focus → valid: border green, trailing check, soft success ring. Never auto-advances focus.
  • Strength meter: 3 bars — Weak (red) · Fair (amber) · Strong (green) — directly under the new-password field.
  • Match error: confirm only, 1.5px red border + semantic fill + inline alert-circle “Passwords don’t match”.
  • Submit gate: “Update password” stays desaturated until strength is met and confirm matches.
  • Required indicator: small red asterisk after each label — never inside the placeholder.
